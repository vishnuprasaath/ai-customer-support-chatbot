COMPANY OVERVIEW
----------------
Neubaitics Tech Private Limited is an Indian technology company specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, and the Internet of Things (IoT). The company is headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, with its registered office in Salem, Tamil Nadu, India. Neubaitics is an Anna University incubated startup, reflecting strong ties to academic research and innovation.

The company's core mission is to bridge the gap between high-level AI research and real-world industrial applications. Neubaitics designs intelligent systems that don't just automate tasks — they understand environments and make autonomous decisions.

Neubaitics operates as both an EdTech provider (training the next generation of AI engineers) and a Tech Solutions firm (building hardware and software for businesses).
